This work involves the development of an interactive video game implemented in the Unity3D environment, using the C# language, titled "Survive The Zombies." The game is compatible with Windows and is designed to entertain the user by continuously challenging their skills (specifically targeting accuracy) while enhancing their agility in navigating the space. The player assumes the role of Jack, a former renowned and experienced soldier who has retired from the battlefield and is called upon to defend, with his life and his ever-trusted pistol as an ally, the Biohazard Containment Unit from the relentless waves of zombies.
The game is a shooter survival/horde mode, inspired by titles such as "Army of One," "Call of Duty/Nazi Zombies," and "Left 4 Dead."
For the development, tools like C# were used to program game logic, and the Unity game engine was utilized for the design of the graphical environment. Specifically, techniques were applied in [e.g., physics-based movement, lighting, sound management] and interaction mechanisms, reward systems, and player progression systems. The implementation seeks to deliver an engaging and dynamic user experience, with challenges that increase in difficulty the longer the player survives.
Overall, the game meets the design goals and offers an enjoyable experience, showcasing the technical and artistic elements utilized in its development.
